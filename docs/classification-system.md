# Classification System

Players are categorized based on structural reliability.

## Parlay Anchor
High role stability and repeatable stat path.
Eligible for parlays and full unit singles.

## Single Bet Only
Stable enough for isolated exposure.
Never used in parlays.

## Monitor Only
Environment is uncertain.
Tracked but not bet.

## No Bet
Role instability or volatile stat path.
Completely ignored.

Confidence tiers are assigned separately:

Tier A – Strong structural agreement
Tier B – Minor volatility present
Tier C – Efficiency dependent
Tier D – Structural fragility
